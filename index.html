    FAQ

    When does Nexa launch? expand_more

    Public launch is planned for late 2026. Founding hosts receive invitations and onboarding before then — expected Late August 2026.

    Who can apply? expand_more

    Anyone who owns or manages accommodation in Morocco — apartments, villas, riads, hotels, hostels, and more.

    How long is the 0% commission? expand_more

    Founding hosts get 0% commission for their first three months after launch.

    Can I list multiple properties? expand_more

    Yes. Tell us how many you'd like to list in the application.

    Do I need Airbnb experience? expand_more

    No. New hosts and experienced hosts are both welcome.

    How will I know if I'm accepted? expand_more

    We'll email approved hosts with next steps before public launch.
